# QoS (Quality of Service)

## Definition
QoS is used to prioritize certain types of network traffic.

## Simple explanation
It helps important traffic, such as voice or video, get better treatment than less important traffic.

## Common goals
- Reduce delay for real-time traffic
- Prevent congestion
- Improve user experience

## Example
A company uses QoS so voice calls keep working well even when file downloads are happening.

## Quick review
QoS gives priority to important traffic.
