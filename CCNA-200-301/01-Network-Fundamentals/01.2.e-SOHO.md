# SOHO Networks

## Definition
SOHO means Small Office/Home Office. A SOHO network is a small network used in a home, remote office, or very small business.

## Simple explanation
A SOHO router often combines many roles into one box: router, switch, firewall, wireless AP, DHCP server, and NAT device.

## Common components
- Internet modem or ONT
- Wireless router
- Built-in Ethernet switch ports
- Wi-Fi clients
- Printers or NAS devices

## Common services
- DHCP for client IP settings.
- NAT/PAT for internet access.
- DNS forwarding.
- Basic firewall filtering.
- Wireless security.

## Design concerns
- Use strong WPA2/WPA3 security.
- Change default admin passwords.
- Keep firmware updated.
- Separate guest Wi-Fi when possible.

## Exam focus
SOHO devices combine multiple enterprise network functions into one small appliance.

## Quick review
SOHO networks are small networks where one device often performs many jobs.
